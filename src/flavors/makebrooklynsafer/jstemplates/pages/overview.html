We want our sidewalks and streets to be safe, so that children can walk or bike to school and play areas. Elementary schools in this area are shown on the map in orange. Please help us by identifying unsafe conditions on a route you might take to school, and giving us suggestions about how to improve them.

{{#_}}About the map{{/_}}

This map was created with Shareabouts from OpenPlans, to demonstrate at the Safe Routes to School National Conference, held in Sacramento from August 13-15, 2013.

The information you share on this map will inform how OpenPlans improves its tools for SRTS, and will also be shared with Walk Sacramento.

Read more about OpenPlans' tools for Safe Routes to Schools projects. If you would like to be kept informed of new tools for SRTS, please include your name and email address with your suggestions to receive an occasional email newsletter from OpenPlans.

{{#_}}Using the map{{/_}}

To begin, click the “Identify a safety issue” button on the bottom of your screen.

Choose the category of your issue or suggestion from the dropdown menu.

Add more detail in the description box. For example, if the category you choose is “intersection issue”, the description might be something like, “driver failed to yield to pedestrian; signage is unclear.”

You can upload a photo to give even more information.

Optionally adding your name and email address will allow us to keep you informed about the progress of this project.

Click Save.

You’re ready to identify another safety issue.
