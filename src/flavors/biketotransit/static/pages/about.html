The Bicycle Coalition of Greater Philadelphia, SEPTA, NJ Transit, PATCO, DVRPC, and Open Plans are collaborating to determine how transit stations can better accommodate bicyclists. This map will help identify where the demand for better bicycle facilities is greatest.

Please click on a station where you would like to see improvements, and respond to the questions that follow. You can choose a station you currently bike to, or one you would bike to if improvements were made. This map will be made available until December 1, 2012.

OpenPlans developed this map using its Shareabouts software, which is a tool to crowdsource data in a neighborhood or city.
